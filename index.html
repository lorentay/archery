About Us

Straight to the Point Archery is a non profit archery education center founded in 2005 by Giap Nguyen, after competing with the US Archery Team in the 2000 Summer Olympics. A father or three, Giap was not satisified with the archery education options availble for his childern. He wanted a place for them to learn valuable life skills through the disipline of archery. He's dedicated Straight to the Point Archery to that mission. From Beginner to Elite, archers of all ages and ability learn about this exciting Olympic and international sport.

Our Facilities

Our state of the art educational and training facility incorporates detailed technical video analysis combined with generations of archery experience from certified coaches and instructors. We have an indoor training facility with 28 shooting lanes and two private coaching rooms. We also have access to 26 additional 20 yard lanes and an outdoor shooting field.

Our Services

  • Sign up for group or private lessons, whether your a first time archer or professional. Our instructors have qualified for level 1 and level 2 and have met the safety requirements of the USA Archery (USAA). Once set up with proper fitting equipment, each archer will be given the basic safety instruction to start and will be introduced to various shooting techniques.
