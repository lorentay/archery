About Straight to the Point Archery

Straight to the Point Archery is a non-profit archery education center founded in 2005 by Giap Nguyen after competing with the US Archery Team in the 2000 Summer Olympics. A father of three, Giap was not satisfied with the archery education options available for his children. He wanted a place for them to learn valuable life skills through the discipline of archery.

"Our mission is for students of all ages and abilities to master archery skills in this exciting Olympic and international sport."

-giap nguyen

Our Facilities

Our state-of-the-art educational and training facility incorporates detailed technical video analysis and generations of archery experience from certified coaches and instructors. We have an indoor training facility with 28 shooting lanes and two private coaching rooms. We also have access to 26 additional 20-yard lanes and an outdoor shooting field.
