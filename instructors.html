Derek Jenkins

Derick is a Washington Native and an experienced outdoorsman with many years hunting, fishing, skiing, canoeing, archery, shooting, and minimalistic camping. is skillfun mastery of bushcraft and wilderness survival knowledge helps define his character. He enjoys handcrafting his own long bows, flint knapped primitive arrows and making custome leather products.

Amy Smith

Amy truly has a passion for archery. She comes from a long line of huners, fisherman, and trappers of Canada. Amy places a strong emphasis on safety and is enthusiastic about teaching. Amy also loves fitness and has been a YMCA group exercise and personal trainer for over 18 years.

Nathan Lee

Nathan is a USA Archery Certified Level 3 NTS coach and has been teaching archery since 2009. He has many years of experience teaching people of all ages, experience gained as a Martial Arts Instructor and Studio Manager.

Brian Smith

Brian is a graduate of Archery Academy, class of 2017. A former boy scout, he developed a passion for archery after breaking his ankle playing little league softball. When he's not out in the field shooting arrows, he likes to kick back with a cold beer and a puzzle. He loves to teach and is looking forward to working with all students.
